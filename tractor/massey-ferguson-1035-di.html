Massey Ferguson 1035 DI

One of India's best-selling entry-level tractors, the 1035 DI runs on a classic 3-cylinder Simpsons engine. It won "Classic Tractor of the Year" at the 2023 ITOTY Awards and remains a low-maintenance favourite for small and marginal farmers.
