Hydraulic tipping trolley

Trolleys are used for hauling produce, fertiliser, and farm material. A hydraulic tipping mechanism (powered off the tractor's hydraulics) saves manual unloading labour — worth the extra cost on most working farms.

TIPPING TROLLEY — TYPICAL SPEC RANGE 2–6 TONNE
Load Capacity 2–6 tonne
Axle Type Single / Tandem
Tipping Hydraulic
Typical Price ₹1.10L–2.80L*

What to check before buying

Tractor lift capacityConfirm your tractor's hydraulic lift capacity supports the loaded trolley weight
Tyre ratingMatch tyre load rating to the trolley's rated capacity, not just the chassis size
Brake typeLarger trolleys (4+ tonne) should have their own braking system, not rely on the tractor alone

*Prices vary by brand, capacity, and axle configuration. Figures are indicative market ranges as of June 2026.
