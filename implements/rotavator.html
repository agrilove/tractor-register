Rotavator

The rotavator is the most common tillage implement bought alongside a new tractor in India — it breaks and levels soil in a single pass, replacing several ploughing-and-harrowing passes. Sizing it correctly to your tractor's PTO HP matters more than most buyers expect.

ROTAVATOR — TYPICAL SPEC RANGE 5–7 FT
Working Width 5–7 ft
Required PTO 35–55 HP
Blade Count 36–48
Typical Price ₹85K–1.45L*

What to check before buying

Gearbox typeSide-drive vs. center-drive — center-drive distributes torque more evenly on wider units
Blade typeL-shaped (general soil) vs. C-shaped (sticky/wet soil)
PTO speed matchMost rotavators are built for 540 RPM PTO — confirm your tractor's PTO speed matches
Skid/depth controlAdjustable skids let you set tillage depth without re-setting the 3-point hitch each time

*Prices vary by brand, width, and blade configuration. Figures are indicative market ranges as of June 2026.
