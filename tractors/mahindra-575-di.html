Mahindra 575 DI

The 575 DI has been one of Mahindra's steadiest sellers for three decades, recently relaunched with updated torque and transmission options. It's a 4-cylinder, 2WD workhorse aimed at the 40-50 HP segment — ploughing, sowing, and haulage on small-to-mid sized farms.
